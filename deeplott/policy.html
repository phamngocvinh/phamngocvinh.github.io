Nature of Service

Deeplott is an analytical tool that processes publicly available data from Vietlott for statistical and probabilistic research purposes. We do not provide gambling services.

Important Disclaimer

Probability analysis is for informational purposes only. Past results do not guarantee future outcomes. Users are responsible for their own financial decisions.

1. Data Collection

Deeplott prioritizes user anonymity. We collect:

  • No Personal Identity: We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • Public Data: We only fetch lottery results that are publicly released by the official operator.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect basic technical data (e.g., device type) to optimize performance, but this is never linked to your identity.

2. Local Processing

Most of the probability calculations and personalized number tracking are performed locally on your device. We do not maintain personal profiles of your "favorite" numbers on our servers.

3. Third-Party Links

Deeplott may display information from official lottery sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites.
